### Key Metrics and Dimensions:

- **Total Sales:** How many sales made in total.
- **Total Order Amount:** How many orders placed in total.
- **Total Quantity:** Total quantity ordered.
- **Gender:** Female & Male.
- **Age:** User Age.
- **Marital Status:** Married or not.
- **Weekday:** Day of week.
- **Month:** Month of year.
